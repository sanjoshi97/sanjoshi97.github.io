I am a PhD Student at the Max Planck Institute for Brain Research in Frankfurt. I work in the Social Systems and Circuits lab. Our lab broadly focuses on studying the neural basis of vocal communication in naked mole-rats. Previous work shows the existance of colony specific dialects. My PhD project specifically aims at understanding how these eusocial mammals develop these signature vocalizations.
